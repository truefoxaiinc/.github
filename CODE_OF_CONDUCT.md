# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a professional, inclusive and harassment-free community for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience or role.

## Expected behavior

- Communicate respectfully and constructively.
- Welcome different viewpoints and experience levels.
- Give and accept actionable technical feedback.
- Protect confidential, personal and customer information.
- Put community safety and project integrity first.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, deliberate disruption, doxxing and publication of private information are unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to **info@truefoxaiinc.com**. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when necessary. Reports will be handled as confidentially as reasonably possible.
